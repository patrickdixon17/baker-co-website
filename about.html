About Baker & Co LLC

Family Heritage. Quality Craftsmanship.

Our Story

Baker & Co LLC is a family-owned business built on generations of craftsmanship and dedication to excellence. What started as a passion for quality electrical work has grown into a full-service electrical and construction company serving our community.

We believe in doing things the right way; no shortcuts, no compromises. Every project we take on is treated with the same care and attention we'd give our own home.

Family Heritage

Our commitment to quality craftsmanship has been passed down through generations. The Baker family has always believed in honest work, fair prices, and treating every customer like family.

Today, we continue that tradition by combining time-tested skills with modern techniques and technology to deliver exceptional results on every project.

Our Values

Quality

We never cut corners. Every job is done right the first time with attention to detail and superior craftsmanship.

Integrity

Honest communication, fair pricing, and transparent processes. You can trust us to do what we say we'll do.

Reliability

We show up on time, stay on schedule, and deliver results you can count on, project after project.

Family

As a family business, we treat our customers like family; with respect, care, and dedication to their satisfaction.
